Constructing Effective Arguments

Public Forum Debate • Module 2 • Lesson 1

Video lesson will appear here

Lesson Overview

In this lesson, you'll learn how to construct effective arguments for Public Forum debate. We'll cover the components of a strong argument, including claims, warrants, and impacts, as well as how to organize your arguments for maximum persuasiveness.

Key Learning Objectives:

  • Understand the structure of an effective argument
  • Learn to develop clear claims supported by evidence
  • Practice creating logical warrants that connect evidence to claims
  • Master the art of impacting arguments to demonstrate significance
